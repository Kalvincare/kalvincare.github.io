Refund Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

At KalvinCare, we stand behind the quality of our fresh pet food and supplements. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we'll make it right.

1. Trial Pack Refunds

3-Day Trial Pack (₹199):

  • Full refund if your pet doesn't like the food
  • Must be requested within 7 days of delivery
  • No questions asked - we trust your feedback
  • Refund processed within 3-5 business days

2. Subscription Plan Refunds

Monthly Plans:

  • First Month: Full refund if not satisfied
  • Subsequent Months: Pro-rated refund for unused portion
  • Cancellation: No refund for current month, but no charges for future months
  • Plan Changes: Pro-rated adjustments for upgrades/downgrades

3. Quality Issues

We offer immediate refunds for:

  • Damaged or spoiled products
  • Incorrect orders or missing items
  • Delivery issues (late, wrong address, etc.)
  • Allergic reactions (with veterinary confirmation)
  • Product defects or contamination

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason
  3. We'll review and process your request
  4. Refund will be issued to original payment method
  5. You'll receive confirmation via email

Processing Time: 3-5 business days for credit/debit cards, 5-7 days for UPI transfers.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Customized products that have been opened/used
  • Products past their expiration date
  • Items damaged due to improper storage
  • Consultation fees (if separate service)
  • Delivery charges for orders outside Bengaluru

6. Return Policy

For Quality Issues:

  • We may request photos of damaged products
  • Returns not required for most refunds
  • If return is needed, we'll arrange pickup
  • No return shipping charges for quality issues

7. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  • Contact our customer service team
  • We'll review your case personally
  • We may offer alternative solutions
  • Our goal is your complete satisfaction

8. Contact Us

To request a refund or discuss any issues:

  • Email: support@kalvincare.com
  • Phone: +91 96320 49639
  • WhatsApp: +91 96320 49639
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
